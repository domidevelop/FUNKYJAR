Mojito

  • 2 ounces light rum
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 6 to 8 fresh mint leaves
  • Splash club soda
  • 1 lime (cut into two halves)
  • Garnish: mint sprig

Place the sugar, mint leaves, and a splash of club soda into a highball glass. Muddle well to dissolve the sugar and to release the flavor of the mint. Squeeze the juice from both halves of a lime into the glass, dropping one-half into the glass. Add the rum. Stir well. Fill the glass with ice cubes. Top with club soda. Garnish with the mint sprig.
